George Carlin

The dean of counterculture comedians

Trivia

  • His dream career as a kid was to become a movie star like his idol Danny Kaye.
  • Carlin's career spanned half a century during which he headlined 14 HBO comedy specials.
  • He appeared on The Tonight Show over 130 times.
  • He is considered one of the most influential stand-up comics of all time.
  • He was court-martialed (more than once) in the air force.
  • Carlin won five Grammys, for his albums FM & AM, Jammin' in New York, Brain Droppings, Napalm & Silly Putty and It's Bad for Ya.
  • Carlin was awarded the 2008 Mark Twain Prize for American Humor by the Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, becoming the first posthumous recipient.
  • Carlin had a history of heart problems spanning three decades ultimately causing his death.
